BMSP · PPKE Budapest · Dec 2025

Cardiac Autonomic Assessment via PPG-Derived HRV During Personalized Sound Stimulation

Modular Python pipeline investigating how self-selected music modulates the Autonomic Nervous System via PPG signals. Recorded 10 participants across baseline, favorite-song, and least-favorite-song conditions. Applied Butterworth filtering, adaptive peak detection, and FFT spectral analysis, then Friedman and Wilcoxon tests plus an individual responsiveness classifier showing every subject had significant autonomic shifts despite non-significant group means.

03
3DVMMC Lab · UAM Madrid · 2025

Calibrated Multi-View 3D Reconstruction

Complete MATLAB pipeline for recovering 3D scene structure from multiple camera views. Covers camera calibration from checkerboard and custom planar patterns, SIFT/SURF/ORB feature detection and matching with RANSAC-based geometric verification, homography and fundamental matrix computation, epipolar geometry visualization, and multi-view sparse reconstruction with bundle adjustment. Achieved 4.47 px reprojection error across 672 retained multi-view tracks.
