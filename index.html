Research areas

Trustworthy vision AI for medical, document, and inspection workflows.

My research direction is centered on computer vision systems that are useful beyond benchmark scores: clear data assumptions, measurable reliability, and explanations that help people understand when a model should or should not be trusted.

Current direction

Cross-modal interpretability and trustworthy vision AI.

My active TRDP at UAM focuses on explaining CLIP's image-text alignment through a Cross-Modal Attention Bridge — bridging vision and text attention rollouts with statistical filtering to produce faithful, text-conditional visual explanations. Alongside this, I work with biomedical imaging, visual inspection, and low-resource OCR where the shared concern is interpretable, reliable model behaviour.

01
BMSP · PPKE Budapest · Dec 2025

Cardiac Autonomic Assessment via PPG-Derived HRV During Personalized Sound Stimulation

Modular Python pipeline investigating how self-selected music modulates the Autonomic Nervous System via PPG signals. Recorded 10 participants across baseline, favorite-song, and least-favorite-song conditions. Applied Butterworth filtering, adaptive peak detection, and FFT spectral analysis, then Friedman and Wilcoxon tests plus an individual responsiveness classifier showing every subject had significant autonomic shifts despite non-significant group means.

02
TRDP 1 · UAM Madrid · Ongoing

Statistically Filtered Cross-Modal Attention for CLIP Interpretability

Cross-Modal Attention Bridge (CMAB) explaining CLIP's image-text alignment via K-sigma statistical filtering on attention rollouts. Generates fine-grained, text-conditional visual explanations per token without modifying the model. Supervised by Prof. Jenny Benois-Pineau (Bordeaux) and Marcos Escudero-Viñolo (UAM).

CLIP ViT PyTorch XAI Attention Rollout Vision-Language
03
3DVMMC Lab · UAM Madrid · 2025

Calibrated Multi-View 3D Reconstruction

Complete MATLAB pipeline for recovering 3D scene structure from multiple camera views. Covers camera calibration from checkerboard and custom planar patterns, SIFT/SURF/ORB feature detection and matching with RANSAC-based geometric verification, homography and fundamental matrix computation, epipolar geometry visualization, and multi-view sparse reconstruction with bundle adjustment. Achieved 4.47 px reprojection error across 672 retained multi-view tracks.
